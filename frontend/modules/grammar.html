Learn Sign Language

Step-by-step guide to basic sentences in sign language.

1. Hello, how are you?

  • Step 1: Sign "Hello" by waving your hand near your forehead.
  • Step 2: Sign "How" by bringing both hands together in a curved shape and rotating them outward.
  • Step 3: Sign "You" by pointing at the person.

2. My name is [Name].

  • Step 1: Sign "My" by placing your hand flat on your chest.
  • Step 2: Sign "Name" by forming an "H" shape with both hands and tapping them together twice.
  • Step 3: Fingerspell your name.

3. I am happy.

  • Step 1: Sign "I" by pointing to yourself.
  • Step 2: Sign "Happy" by brushing your hands upward on your chest.

4. Where is the bathroom?

  • Step 1: Sign "Where" by shaking your index finger side to side.
  • Step 2: Sign "Bathroom" by forming a "T" shape and shaking it.

5. I need help.

  • Step 1: Sign "I" by pointing to yourself.
  • Step 2: Sign "Need" by forming an "X" shape and moving it downward.
  • Step 3: Sign "Help" by placing your dominant hand on your non-dominant hand and lifting it slightly.

6. What is your name?

  • Step 1: Sign "What" by holding both hands open and moving them side to side.
  • Step 2: Sign "Your" by pointing to the person.
  • Step 3: Sign "Name" by forming an "H" shape with both hands and tapping them together twice.

7. I like ice cream.

  • Step 1: Sign "I" by pointing to yourself.
  • Step 2: Sign "Like" by touching your thumb and middle finger together and pulling them away from your chest.
  • Step 3: Sign "Ice Cream" by mimicking the motion of holding and licking an ice cream cone.

8. Thank you.

  • Step 1: Sign "Thank You" by touching your fingers to your lips and extending them outward.

9. I am learning sign language.

  • Step 1: Sign "I" by pointing to yourself.
  • Step 2: Sign "Learning" by mimicking the motion of picking up knowledge from your palm to your forehead.
  • Step 3: Sign "Sign Language" by mimicking the motion of signing with both hands.

10. Goodbye, see you later.

  • Step 1: Sign "Goodbye" by waving your hand.
  • Step 2: Sign "See You Later" by pointing to your eye and then forming an "L" shape with your hand.
